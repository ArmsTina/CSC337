Korean Ex-Pro Player/Coach, DONGYOUNG "Arms_Tina" YANG

I want to introduce a Korean player I know very well. Actually, it's me! I was involved in Esports for about 4 years, as both an amateur and professional player, as well as a coach.

  1. 2018 ~ 2020: Several Amateur Teams
  2. 2020 ~ 2021: Pro Esports Team, SGA Incheon
  3. 2022 ~ 2023: Esports Team at Western Michigan University

As a player, I usually took on the roles of Support and Breacher. In the first Korean Amateur League, my team won 1st place, and later, as a professional team coach, we achieved 1st place again in the Korean Pro League.
