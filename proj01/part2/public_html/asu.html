Arizona State University (ASU), located in Tempe, Arizona, is a public research university known for its innovation and large student body. ASU is one of the largest public universities in the United States, offering a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across its multiple campuses. The university is dedicated to research, entrepreneurship, and community impact, with a strong emphasis on sustainability and public service. ASU’s sports teams, known as the Sun Devils, compete in the NCAA Division I Pac-12 Conference. ASU operates as a leader in global education, partnering with institutions worldwide to provide students with diverse and innovative learning experiences.
